About Me

AZENSHA is a singer, songwriter and producer coming from a small town in Uttar Pradesh, currently based in New Delhi. In October of 2022, she released her debut album titled "AZENSHA" which she wrote, composed and sang in its entirety. Having struggled with mental health issues , her neurodivergent brain takes you on a journey of understanding pain through the neglected lens of compassion. The artwork-from start to finish-is an individual's enterprise of running through a dark tunnel which may feel endless but always has a light at the end of it.

Read more about me

She is a storyteller as she creates a picture not just through her lyrics or melodies but has also found her voice through visual media, having directed all her music videos herself. She's a psychology, literature and spirituality enthusiast who is mixing it all up and letting it out through her music. A trained classical vocalist who knows her ways from jazz to thumri - from Ghazal to Pop , she is a vocal gymnast who is also to be held responsible for the words that she sings as she has been writing songs from the age of 12. Though still venturing in the field of film-making, she is a storyteller who dares to explore artforms that excite her.
